F.A.Q.

How to add an event

  1. Go to the calendar page.
  2. Click on a date.
  3. Write the name of the event.
  4. Click save.

How to delete an event

  1. Go to the calendar page.
  2. Click on an event you want to delete.
  3. Click delete.

Where can i search for existing accounts

  1. Hover on the arrow "on the top right side of the page".
  2. Click on wishlist.
  3. on the right side there is a search bar, where you can search for existing accounts.

How to earn tokens to spin the luckywheel

  1. Follow our instagram account.

Where can i find possible gifts for my friends or family members

  1. Hover on the arrow "on the top right side of the page".
  2. Click on wishlist.
  3. on the right side there is a search bar, where you can search for existing accounts.
  4. When you find an account you can click on it, to view their wishlist.
